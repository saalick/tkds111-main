Comprehensive Syllabus

Tadika Gemilang Suria provides a nurturing environment where children learn through play, exploration, and creativity. Our program blends bilingual language learning, early math and science discovery, daily music and piano practice, and expressive arts. By engaging in diverse activities, children grow with confidence, curiosity, and joy — preparing them for a bright future.
