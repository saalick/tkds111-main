Our Courses — What We Offer

A balanced, activity-based curriculum combining bilingual language learning, creative arts, music, early numeracy & science, computational thinking, and outdoor exploration. Expand a course to see details, activities and outcomes.

Languages — English, Mandarin & Bahasa Malaysia

Ages 2–6Bilingual foundation • Daily language exposure

Overview: A structured language program using thematic activities to build vocabulary, listening and speaking confidence across English, Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia. Activities use songs, storytelling and role-play.

  • Daily small-group language sessions
  • Thematic vocabulary practice (themes link across subjects)
  • Storytelling, puppet theatre & conversational games
  • Parent-child reading workshops
Details
Session length
20–40 min daily
Learning Outcomes
Early fluency, listening skills, confidence in speaking

Early Mathematics & Science

Ages 3–6Hands-on discovery • Problem solving

Overview: Play-based math and science that introduces numbers, patterns, measurement and simple experiments. Our thematic approach connects math & science with real-world examples.

  • Counting games, sorting & simple measurements
  • Nature-based science experiments (plant growth, water play)
  • Math competitions & project tasks
  • Logical puzzles that build problem-solving
Details
Session length
40–50 min (twice weekly)
Learning Outcomes
Numeracy readiness, observation skills, scientific curiosity

Music & Piano (Compulsory)

Ages 3–6Daily short individual lessons

Overview: Every child gets a short individual piano session each school day, plus group music & movement classes. This builds concentration, rhythm, and creative expression.

  • Daily 5–10 minute individual piano practice
  • Group singing, percussion & movement
  • Seasonal concerts & recital practice
  • Music-integrated learning (themes + songs)
Details
Session length
Daily short practice + weekly group class
Learning Outcomes
Attention, fine motor control, confidence in performance

Creative Arts & Craft

Ages 2–6Drawing, painting & drama

Overview: A rich arts program including drawing, painting, sculpture, crafts and drama. Structured play and teacher-guided projects bring thematic topics to life.

  • Project-based art themes tied to class topics
  • Drama & puppet sessions to encourage storytelling
  • Crafts that develop hand-eye coordination
  • Annual drawing competitions & exhibitions
Details
Session length
40–50 min (2–3 times weekly)
Learning Outcomes
Creativity, fine motor skills, self-expression

Computational Thinking

Ages 4–6Logic, sequencing & block coding

Overview: Introduction to computational thinking using unplugged activities and block-based coding tools. Focus on sequencing, pattern recognition and problem decomposition.

  • Unplugged logic games & puzzles
  • Visual/block-based coding activities
  • Step-by-step project creation (simple robots, animations)
  • Computational thinking integrated in thematic projects
Details
Session length
30–45 min weekly
Learning Outcomes
Logical thinking, sequencing, basic algorithmic awareness

All AgesCharacter, routines & social skills

  • Teacher-guided social skills practice
