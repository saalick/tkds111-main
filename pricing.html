Which Course Fits Your Child?

Every child learns differently. Here’s a guide to help parents choose the right course for their child’s age and developmental stage.

Ages 2–6

Languages (English, Mandarin, BM)

Best for young learners beginning to build communication skills. Trilingual exposure builds early fluency and confidence.

  • Storytelling
  • Daily reading
  • Puppet shows
Ages 3–6

Early Math & Science

Perfect for curious children who enjoy discovering patterns and asking “why.” Builds problem-solving and logical thinking.

  • Counting games
  • Nature experiments
  • Projects
Ages 3–6

Music & Piano

Recommended for children developing focus and rhythm. Daily practice enhances concentration and creativity.

  • Piano practice
  • Group music
  • Concert prep
Ages 2–6

Creative Arts & Craft

Great for expressive children who enjoy drawing and storytelling. Boosts creativity and fine motor skills.

  • Painting
  • Drama
  • Competitions
Ages 4–6

Computational Thinking

Ideal for children who like puzzles and building. Introduces logic, coding basics, and problem-solving.

  • Block coding
  • Logic games
  • Robotics
All Ages

Outdoor & Indoor Activities

Best for energetic learners. Develops social skills, teamwork, and motor abilities through play.

  • Sports
  • Sand play
  • Dance
All Ages

Moral & General Studies

For every child to learn empathy, discipline, and cooperation. Builds values and emotional growth.

  • Stories
  • Social tasks
  • Parent workshops
Monthly

Parenting & Teacher Training

For parents and teachers seeking growth. Ensures consistent learning quality and strong home-school links.

  • Workshops
  • Teacher PD
  • Practical tips
